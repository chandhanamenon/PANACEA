Amidst the on-going coronavirus pandemic we at Panacea offer assistance with respect to various things such as follows:


Covid-19 statistics :

While we worry about the situation in our country there are better/worse situations around the world in many countries. Here we provide statistics of each country , continent wise. Most accurate information with live updation is produced.


Doctor on call :

While there are several thousand doctors and nurses serving our fellow people who are affected by coronavirus and trying to make our country corona-free, there are several different health issues that need attention too. And during the times of any health emergency you may contact the relevant doctors as their information is provided here.


Live News Updates :

We may often observe various news channels weaving stories with one fact and many lies, and we end up wasting time and don’t even gain relevant facts. Hence, we at panacea provide live news updates i.e., we provide links to most accurate sources of information which saves lot of your time.


Emergency Contacts :

In times of an emergency specific to covid-19 you may contact the relevant person. Their contact information is specified on our site.


Did You Know :

There are some really good measures taken by our government to monitor the current situation and take decisions that are in the best interest of all citizens. Want to know what some of these are? Check it out on our site


Speeches :

From a chaiwala (tea-seller) to the prime minister of our nation , Narendra Modi ji has come long way to achieve his dream and support, motivate the people of this country to achieve theirs as well. He proved his efficiency during a time like this, and acted very maturely and saved the day for all of us. Links to some of his very inspiring /informative speeches are found on this site .


Charity on wheels :

If you are viewing this site right now, it means you have access to basic necessities of life like food and shelter. But not a lot of them are as privileged as us. So let’s join our hands to help them. Please donate anything you can to the donation boxes installed in your area. A person concerned will collect all the goods and distribute it to the less privileged.
